# gw2-oops-wrong-template-releases

A mod for Guild Wars 2 with nexus loader. Shows a compact window with your gear and build templates.
- Optionally show weapons, sigils, and relic of your gear templates on hover
- Optionally show skills and traits of your build templates on hover
- Optionally show the boon (alac/quick) provided by a build, based on traits picked.
- Right click on a template to link it with another. When a template is linked and clicked, it will also load the linked one.
